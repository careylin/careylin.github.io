# Denali Design System

A comprehensive design system for AllTrails, powering outdoor experiences across web and mobile platforms.

Denali is AllTrails' design system that brings consistency and efficiency to our product development process. Named after North America's highest peak, it represents our commitment to setting high standards in outdoor technology.

Key achievements:
- Unified design language across web, iOS, and Android platforms
- 60% faster component implementation time
- Improved accessibility compliance to WCAG 2.1 AA standards
- Reduced design inconsistencies by 80%
- Successfully launched across AllTrails Pro and free tier products
